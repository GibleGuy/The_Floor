HELP & SHORTCUTS

How to Play

Objective: Identify the images shown on screen before your time runs out!

Gameplay:

  • Two players take turns answering questions
  • Each player starts with 45 seconds on their timer
  • Type the correct answer to move to the next question
  • When you get an answer correct, the turn switches to the other player
  • You can pass on a question, but you'll wait 3 seconds (timer keeps running)
  • If your timer reaches 0, you lose and the other player wins!

Tips:

  • Answers are case-insensitive
  • You can pass by pressing Enter with an empty input, or press `
  • Use time boosts in customization for an extra 5 seconds
